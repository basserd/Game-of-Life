    Cell rules

1.  Alive cell:
    Whenever a cell has less than 2 alive neighbours.
    (die)

2.  Alive cell:
    Whenever a cell has 2 or 3 neighbours.
    (stay alive): at least live till the next generation.

3.  Alive cell:
    Whenever a cell has more than 3 neighbours.
    (die)

4.  Dead cell:
    Whenever a DEAD cell has 3 neighbours it will be born
    (spawn)
